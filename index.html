About Me

Hello. My name is Karishma Kothari, and I am a student at Atlas Skilltech University, I am currently studying Communication Design, with a minor in Digital Marketing. I was born and raised in Kochi, Kerala and Moved to Mumbai for my studies. My first step into design was with Photography, I learned how I liked creating and capturing things through Photography. I'm calling my Portfolio "My Ikigai" because I believe that design helped me get out of my comfort zone. Though I have much to learn and improve, I'd like to think creating and visualising is my sense of purpose.
